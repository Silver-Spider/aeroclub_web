Our Mission

Founded in 2018, AeroBMSCE has been dedicated to bringing together enthusiasts of aeromodelling, drones, and RC planes. Our passion is to create a community where innovation meets the sky.

Building a Community of Flight Enthusiasts

At SkyMasters, we believe that the sky is not the limit, but just the beginning. Our community brings together hobbyists, professionals, and innovators who share a passion for aerial technology and flight.

We provide resources, training, and events that help our members develop their skills, showcase their creations, and push the boundaries of what's possible in aeromodelling.

Our Journey

2018

Founded by a group of 5 aviation enthusiasts from BMSCE with a shared vision to create an inclusive CLUB for aerial hobbyists.

2020

Expanded to 10+ members and hosted our first regional drone racing championship with participants from different states.

2022

Launched our exclusive Club, introducing aeromodelling to over 200+ students across the entire college.

2025

Today, with over 50 members, we're pioneering new technologies and participating in national competitions that push the boundaries of aerial innovation.

Meet the Squadrons

Our community is organized into specialized teams, each focusing on different aspects of aerial technology and flight.

Team Alpha

Specializing in high-performance racing drones and competitive flight techniques.

Team Beta

Focused on aerial photography, cinematography, and professional imaging techniques.

Team Charlie

Engineering and innovation team developing cutting-edge drone, UAVs and RC technologies.

Team Delta

Youth education and community outreach, bringing aerial technology to the next generation.
